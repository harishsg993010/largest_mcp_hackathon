About OIDC-A

OpenID Connect for Agents (OIDC-A) 1.0 is an extension to OpenID Connect Core 1.0 that provides a framework for representing, authenticating, and authorizing LLM-based agents within the OAuth 2.0 ecosystem.

As LLM-based agents become increasingly prevalent in digital ecosystems, there is a growing need for standardized methods to represent their identity and manage their authorization. OIDC-A addresses these challenges by defining:

  • Standard claims for agent identity and capabilities
  • Mechanisms for agent attestation and verification
  • Protocols for delegation chains and authority management
  • Discovery mechanisms for agent capabilities

Our platform implements the full OIDC-A 1.0 specification, providing a secure and standardized way to integrate AI agents into your authentication ecosystem.
